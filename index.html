Bangladesh

A small country of south asia. It's area is 147,570 km. Dhaka is the capital of Bangladesh.

It has many history of its independence from pakistan.The language movement happend in 1952.The declaration of independence in 26th march in 1971.The victory day was 16th December 1971.

The economy of bangladesh is based on agirculture. Agriculture is the provider of a major portion of total GDP.
